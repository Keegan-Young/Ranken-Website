Welcome to Ranken

Ranken Technical College is a leading Missouri technical college, and a smart choice for anyone motivated to build a successful career. Ranken students don’t just graduate with a tech school degree – they graduate ready to work.

Your career is our priority and it shows. Tech schools give you the training and skills you need to land the job you want – and many Ranken grads land jobs within six months!

With more hands-on training than anyone else, programs for only the most in-demand technical careers, and strong relationships with major employers, a Ranken education helps you launch or advance a successful career in the field you choose.
